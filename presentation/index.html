Complexity & Creativity

Going beyond simple boxes

Procedural Geometry

V-Shape Hull: Custom drawVBlock() with manually calculated vertex normals for a tapered keel.

Curved Stern: GL_TRIANGLE_FAN generated via sin(rad) & cos(rad) loops.

Physics & Environment

Wave Math: y = sin(x) + cos(z). Summing two waves creates non-repetitive interference.

Buoyancy: Ship's Y-position binds to the same water equation.

Background: Reusable drawPyramid() function for mountains.
